Explore Arizona State University

Arizona State University is one of the largest and most innovative public universities in the United States, known for its strong emphasis on research, inclusivity, and real-world problem solving. Located primarily in Tempe, ASU offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across fields such as engineering, business, sustainability, and the arts. With a diverse student body and a focus on accessibility, ASU fosters an environment where students are encouraged to collaborate, think creatively, and prepare for global challenges. Arizona State University has multiple campuses across the Phoenix area, including its main campus in Tempe, along with additional campuses in Downtown Phoenix, West Phoenix, and Polytechnic, each offering a different atmosphere and academic focus.
